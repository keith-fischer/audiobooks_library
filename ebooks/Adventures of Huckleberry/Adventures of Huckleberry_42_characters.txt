Tom Sawyer	male	The main protagonist of the story, a clever and adventurous boy who helps Jim escape from slavery. He is proud, joyful, and full of mischief. He pretends to be Sid while in the hospital.
Huck Finn	male	The narrator of the story, a mischievous and independent boy who helps Tom plan Jim's escape. He is resourceful and loyal, though he struggles with his upbringing and the moral complexities of helping a runaway slave.
Jim	*male	A runaway slave who is freed by Tom and Huck. He is loyal, grateful, and believes he has a hairy breast that predicts his wealth.
Aunt Polly	female	Tom's aunt who raises him and is the mother of Sid. She is strict, caring, and initially angry at Tom for his pranks but later shows affection.
Aunt Sally	female	Tom's aunt who is also his guardian. She is emotional, protective of her family, and initially believes Tom has been a bad influence.
*Uncle Silas	male	Tom's uncle who is confused and overwhelmed by the events of the story. He becomes disoriented after learning about Tom's deception.
Old Doctor	*male	A kind and caring physician who helps treat Tom after he is shot. He defends Jim's actions and praises his loyalty.
*Sid	*male	Tom's younger brother who is mistaken for Tom by Aunt Sally. He is portrayed as a good-natured boy.
*Old Miss Watson	female	The late owner of Jim who freed him in her will. She was ashamed to sell him and wanted to set him free.
