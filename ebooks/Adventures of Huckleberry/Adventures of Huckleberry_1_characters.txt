*Tom Sawyer	male	Main protagonist of the story, Tom Sawyer is a mischievous boy who has discovered treasure with Huck Finn. He is adventurous and enjoys playing robberies, having recently proposed to Huck that they start a band of robbers.
*Huckleberry Finn	*male	The narrator of the story, Huck Finn is a young boy who has discovered treasure with Tom Sawyer. He prefers freedom and adventure over civilization, having previously run away from the Widow Douglas's house.
*Aunt Polly	female	Tom Sawyer's aunt who raised him. She is portrayed as a kind but somewhat overly moralistic woman, described as telling the truth mainly in the book.
*Mary	female	Aunt Polly's daughter, mentioned alongside Aunt Polly and the Widow Douglas as characters in The Adventures of Tom Sawyer.
*Widow Douglas	female	A kind woman who takes Huck Finn in and tries to 'civilize' him. She is described as a regular, decent woman who makes Huck feel uncomfortable with her strict rules and lifestyle.
Miss Watson	female	The Widow Douglas's sister who lives with her and takes a strict interest in teaching Huck Finn. She is described as a slim old maid wearing goggles who is very moralistic and strict about Huck's behavior.
*Judge Thatcher	*male	A judge who manages the money that Tom and Huck found in the cave, investing it so they receive interest.
