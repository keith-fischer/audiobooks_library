Arthur	*male	The rightful king of England, son of Uther and Arthur's father. A young man of fifteen years who is fair, tall, comely, strong, gentle in manner, and accomplished in knightly exercises. He draws the sword from the stone, proving his right to rule.
*Uther	*male	Arthur's father, the former king of England who died before Arthur knew his true parentage. He was a powerful ruler whose death led to the succession crisis.
Merlin	*male	A powerful wizard who guides Arthur's rise to power, arranging for Arthur to be raised by Sir Ector and ensuring his true parentage remains hidden until the sword test.
Ector	*male	Arthur's foster father, a knight who raised Arthur as his own son. He discovers Arthur's true parentage and becomes loyal to him after the sword test.
*Key	*male	Arthur's foster-brother, recently knighted. He finds Arthur at the sword stone and claims it as his own before revealing that Arthur pulled it for him.
