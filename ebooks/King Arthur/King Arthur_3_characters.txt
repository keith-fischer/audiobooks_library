King Arthur	male	The main protagonist, king of Britain who drives the Saxons from his realm. He is a brave warrior who fights battles, leads armies, and has a prophetic dream about fiery griffins and serpents. He is known for his strength, honor, and leadership.
*Ryence	male	King of North Wales who makes war upon King Leodegrance. He is Arthur's enemy and is defeated by Arthur in battle.
*King Leodegrance	male	King of Camelgard who is attacked by Ryence. He is a close ally of Arthur and becomes the father of Guinevere.
King Ban	male	One of Arthur's companions who joins him in battle against Ryence. He later departs to deal with King Claudas.
*King Bors	male	One of Arthur's companions who joins him in battle against Ryence. He later departs to deal with King Claudas.
*King Claudas	male	A king who causes mischief and is a threat to Arthur's realm. He is the brother of King Lot.
*Guinevere	female	Daughter of King Leodegrance and future wife of King Arthur. She is first seen at a festival in Camelgard.
*Belisent	*female	King Arthur's half-sister, wife to King Lot. She comes to Caerleon as a messenger but is actually spying on Arthur's power.
*King Lot	male	Belisent's husband and King Arthur's enemy. He is jealous of Arthur because of his relationship with Belisent.
*Gowain	male	Belisent's son and one of four sons who accompany her to Caerleon.
*Gaheris	male	Belisent's son and one of four sons who accompany her to Caerleon.
*Agravaine	male	Belisent's son and one of four sons who accompany her to Caerleon.
*Gareth	male	Belisent's son and one of four sons who accompany her to Caerleon.
*King Pellinore	male	A knight who pursues the questing beast and later fights King Arthur in a duel. He is revealed to be a great knight who will serve Arthur well.
*Colgrin	male	A Saxon king who is defeated by Arthur and besieged in York.
*Baldulph	male	Colgrin's brother who comes to raise the siege of York. He disguises himself as a jester and enters Arthur's camp.
*Cheldric	male	A Saxon leader who lands with six hundred ships on the eastern coast. He is eventually hunted down and killed by Cador.
*Hoel	male	King of the Armorican Britons who comes to help Arthur against the Saxons.
*Cador	male	Duke of Cornwall who pursues and kills Cheldric and other Saxons after they flee from the siege.
*King of Ireland	*male	A king who comes to relieve the besieged Saxons but is defeated by Arthur.
*Nirles	male	A knight who is killed by the mysterious knight at the fountain. His squire seeks justice for his death.
Griflet	*male	A young squire who is knighted by Arthur to avenge his master Nirles's death. He is defeated in combat but survives.
Merlin	*male	A mysterious figure who appears as both a child and an old man. He prophesies Arthur's death, reveals his true identity to him, and helps guide the king.
*King of Rome	*male	An emperor who sends ambassadors demanding tribute from Arthur. He is insulted when Arthur refuses to pay tribute.
