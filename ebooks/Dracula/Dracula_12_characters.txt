Dr. Seward	male	A physician who keeps a diary and is deeply involved in Lucy's medical care and the supernatural events surrounding her death. He is a key figure in the investigation of Lucy's condition.
Van Helsing	male	A Dutch doctor and expert in the supernatural who arrives at Hillingham to help with Lucy's case. He is stern, knowledgeable, and takes charge of the situation.
Lucy Westenra	female	The main female character who becomes a vampire. She is described as beautiful and dies from a supernatural affliction, with her mother also dying before her.
*Mrs. Westenra	female	Lucy's mother who dies of heart disease and is found dead in her room with wounds on her throat.
Arthur Holmwood	male	Lucy's fiancé who arrives at Hillingham to see her and later becomes a key figure in the supernatural investigation.
Quincey Morris	male	A friend of Arthur who offers to help with Lucy's care and later becomes involved in the supernatural events.
Mina Harker	female	Lucy's friend who writes letters to her and later becomes involved in the supernatural investigation. She is married to Jonathan Harker.
*Jonathan Harker	male	Mina's husband who is also involved in the supernatural investigation. He was previously a partner of Mr. Hawkins.
Mr. Hawkins	*male	A wealthy benefactor who adopted Jonathan Harker and left him a fortune. He dies suddenly, causing Mina great sorrow.
Patrick Hennessey	male	A doctor who works at the asylum and provides reports on Renfield's condition. He is responsible for treating patients there.
*Renfield	*male	A patient at the asylum who has violent outbursts and shows signs of vampirism. He is described as having fits and being dangerous.
Jack Smollet	male	A carrier who encounters Renfield and is threatened by him. He works for Harris & Sons Moving and Shipment Company.
