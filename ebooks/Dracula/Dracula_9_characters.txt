Mina Harker	female	A young woman who is engaged to Jonathan Harker and later becomes his wife. She is described as kind, devoted, and a good nurse who cares for her sick fiancé in Buda-Pesth. She is intelligent and determined, keeping a secret note-book from her husband that she vows never to open.
Lucy Westenra	female	A young woman who is engaged to Arthur Holmwood and later becomes his wife. She is described as beautiful, lively, and beloved by her fiancé. She suffers from unexplained illness and weakness in the chapter, with symptoms including pale appearance, breathing difficulties, and nightmares.
Jonathan Harker	male	A young man who is engaged to Mina Harker and later becomes her husband. He appears physically weak, pale, and mentally disturbed after a terrible shock that has left him with brain fever. He is described as having lost his former dignity and resolution.
Sister Agatha	female	A kind and devoted nurse who cares for Jonathan Harker in Buda-Pesth. She is described as a good creature and born nurse who keeps secrets about Jonathan's condition.
Arthur Holmwood	male	A young man who is engaged to Lucy Westenra and later becomes her husband. He is described as devoted to Lucy, concerned about her health, and protective of her wellbeing.
Dr. Seward	male	A physician who is a close friend of Arthur Holmwood and the doctor treating Lucy Westenra. He keeps detailed diary entries about his patients, particularly Renfield and Lucy.
Renfield	*male	A patient in Dr. Seward's asylum who exhibits strange behavior, including violent outbursts and a fixation on consuming living creatures. He has periods of calmness that correspond with moon phases.
*Mother	*female	Lucy Westenra's mother, who is described as being unwell and concerned about her daughter's health. She has heart problems according to her own words.
Van Helsing	male	A renowned physician and scientist from Amsterdam who is called to help diagnose Lucy Westenra's mysterious illness. He is described as highly intelligent, experienced in obscure diseases, and possessing an open mind.
*Miss Murray	*female	A woman who found Lucy Westenra walking in her sleep at East Cliff during their time at Whitby. She is described as a friend of Lucy's who helped care for her.
