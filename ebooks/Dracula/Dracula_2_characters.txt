Jonathan Harker	male	A solicitor's clerk who has just passed his examination and is traveling to explain the purchase of a London estate to Count Dracula. He is observant, thoughtful, and increasingly uneasy about his situation in the castle.
Count Dracula	male	The vampire lord who owns the castle and is hosting Jonathan Harker. He is described as having a strong, aquiline face with sharp features, pale skin, pointed ears, and long fingernails. He speaks English with a strange intonation but shows great strength and has an interest in learning English culture.
*Peter Hawkins	male	A friend of the Count who is involved in arranging Jonathan Harker's visit to purchase an estate in London. He is mentioned as being in Exeter and working with Jonathan Harker on legal matters.
*The Driver	*male	A strong man who drives Jonathan Harker to the castle. He is described as having prodigious strength and a cold, icy hand that makes Jonathan wince.
