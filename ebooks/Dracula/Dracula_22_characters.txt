Jonathan Harker	male	A solicitor and the narrator of the diary. He is married to Mina and deeply devoted to her. He is brave but struggles with despair during the crisis.
Mina Harker	female	Jonathan's wife, a brave and devoted woman who is deeply affected by the supernatural events. She insists on being kept in full confidence of their mission.
Dr. Van Helsing	male	An elderly Dutch doctor who is the intellectual leader of their group. He is wise, determined, and has extensive knowledge about supernatural matters.
*Dr. Seward	male	A doctor who works with Van Helsing and Jonathan. He is practical and concerned about legal procedures and medical matters.
*Renfield	*male	A patient in Dr. Seward's asylum who has been possessed by the vampire Count. He is found dead with a bruised and crushed face.
Lord Godalming	male	A wealthy friend of Jonathan who helps organize their efforts. He is resourceful and takes charge of coordinating the search for other lairs.
Quincey Morris	male	A friend of Jonathan who is practical and resourceful. He suggests using cabs for transportation to avoid drawing attention.
