Dr. Seward	male	A physician who keeps a phonograph diary and works at a lunatic asylum. He is compassionate, dedicated to his patients, and becomes involved in investigating supernatural events related to Lucy's death. He is described as having a noble nature and being deeply affected by the tragic events.
Mina Harker	female	A woman who is married to Jonathan Harker and becomes deeply involved in investigating the supernatural events surrounding Lucy's death. She is intelligent, determined, and shows great courage when facing terrifying truths about the vampire Count Dracula. She is described as sweet-faced, dainty-looking, and has a strong sense of duty.
Jonathan Harker	male	Mina's husband, a young man who travels to Whitby and London to investigate the mysterious arrival of boxes from Varna. He is described as clever, energetic, and full of nerve. He shows remarkable courage by entering the vampire's house twice to investigate.
*Lucy	female	A young woman who dies from a vampire attack and becomes one herself. She is described as very dear to Mina, and her death serves as the central tragedy that drives the investigation. She is portrayed as sweet, kind, and beloved by those around her.
Van Helsing	*male	A professor who arrives in London to help investigate the supernatural events. He is described as a wise, knowledgeable figure with an understanding of vampire lore and supernatural phenomena.
Lord Godalming	male	A friend of Lucy who is deeply affected by her death. He shows great emotional vulnerability and gratitude when comforted by Mina, demonstrating his deep love for Lucy. He is described as a gentleman who has suffered greatly from the loss.
Mr. Morris	male	A friend of Lucy who is also deeply affected by her death. He shows great bravery and compassion, particularly when comforting Lord Godalming. He is described as a brave and unselfish soul who proves himself to be a true friend.
Renfield	*male	A patient at Dr. Seward's asylum who has become obsessed with the vampire Count Dracula. He is described as having periods of madness and sanity, showing signs that he has some connection to the vampire's activities.
*Billington	*male	A local official in Whitby who provides information about the arrival of boxes from Varna. He is described as courteous and helpful in assisting Jonathan Harker with his investigation.
