Dr. Seward	*male	A physician and diary keeper who is initially skeptical of Van Helsing's theories about vampirism. He is loyal to his friend Arthur and becomes convinced of the supernatural events after witnessing Lucy's empty coffin and later her transformation. He is torn between his scientific mind and the reality of what he experiences.
Van Helsing	*male	A Dutch physician and expert on supernatural phenomena who is determined to prove Lucy is a vampire. He is methodical, knowledgeable about ancient folklore and vampire lore, and displays great emotional strength despite his personal grief. He is the driving force behind the investigation.
*Lucy Westenra	female	A young woman who has died and become a vampire (Un-Dead). She was bitten by a vampire while sleepwalking, leading to her death and subsequent transformation. Her body appears beautiful but is actually undead.
Arthur Holmwood	*male	Lord Godalming, Lucy's fiancé who is devastated by her death and transformation. He initially rejects Van Helsing's claims but eventually agrees to help investigate after being convinced of the supernatural truth. He is deeply protective of Lucy's memory.
Quincey Morris	*male	An American friend of Arthur who is willing to help in the investigation. He shows trust in Van Helsing and supports his actions, even when they seem extreme.
Dr. Vincent	*male	A physician at the North Hospital who treats a child with mysterious bite wounds similar to Lucy's. He attributes the bites to animals like bats or rats, showing a more scientific approach than Van Helsing.
*The Child	*male	A young child who was bitten by what appears to be a vampire, showing wounds similar to Lucy's. The child is found in the hospital and later discovered to have been taken by a supernatural entity.
