The Duke	*male	A con artist and fraud who works with the King. He is clever and resourceful, dressing Jim up as a sick Arab to avoid being tied up all day. He pretends to be from England and plays the role of a deaf and dumb person.
The King	*male	A con artist and fraud who works with the Duke. He pretends to be Reverend Elexander Blodgett from England and is skilled at playing the role of a grieving relative. He speaks with an English accent.
Jim	*male	A runaway slave who is dressed up as a sick Arab by the Duke. He wears King Lear's outfit, white horse-hair wig and whiskers, and is painted blue to look like he has been drowned. He is tired of being tied up all day.
Huckleberry	*male	The narrator of the story, a young boy who helps the Duke and King with their schemes. He is tasked with paddling the canoe and assisting with their plans.
Adolphus	*male	A young man who is mistaken for Mr. Wilks by the King and Duke. He is traveling to Orleans on a ship bound for Rio Janeiro, and has a red-headed sister named Mary Jane.
