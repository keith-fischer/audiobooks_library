Miss Watson	female	The widow who owns the garden where the story begins. She has a large black servant named Jim.
Jim	male	Miss Watson's large black servant who works in the kitchen. He is described as a 'big nigger' and becomes a central figure in the story after being tricked by Tom and Huck.
Tom Sawyer	male	The main protagonist who organizes the gang and leads the adventure. He is imaginative, creative, and loves pirate and robber stories.
Huck Finn	male	The narrator and Tom's friend who joins the gang. He is practical, cautious, and more realistic than Tom.
*Jo Harper	*male	A boy who joins the gang with Tom and Huck. He is elected second captain of the Gang.
Ben Rogers	*male	A boy who joins the gang and participates in planning their robberies. He questions some of Tom's ideas about ransom.
*Little Tommy Barnes	*male	A young boy who joins the gang but falls asleep and gets scared when woken up. He wants to go home.
