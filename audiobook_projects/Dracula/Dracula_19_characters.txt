Jonathan Harker	male	A solicitor who is deeply involved in the supernatural events surrounding Count Dracula. He is married to Mina and serves as a key figure in the investigation, documenting events in his journal.
*Mina Harker	female	Jonathan's wife, a strong and intelligent woman who becomes central to the supernatural events. She is kept out of the dangerous aspects of the investigation due to her gender and emotional sensitivity.
Dr. John Seward	male	A physician and close friend of Jonathan, who keeps a diary documenting the events. He is involved in treating Renfield and investigating the supernatural occurrences.
Dr. Van Helsing	male	A Dutch physician and expert on the supernatural who leads the investigation against Count Dracula. He is wise, experienced, and serves as a mentor figure.
Lord Godalming	male	A wealthy young man who is part of the group investigating Dracula. He brings practical skills and resources to the mission.
Quincey Morris	male	A friend and companion who joins the group in their quest to stop Dracula. He is resourceful, brave, and skilled with weapons.
*Mr. Renfield	*male	A patient in Dr. Seward's care who has a disturbing obsession with consuming living creatures and appears to have some connection to Dracula.
*Count Dracula	*male	The main antagonist, a vampire with supernatural powers who is the source of all the horror and danger in the story.
