Dr. Seward	*male	A doctor and diary keeper who is present during the events in the churchyard. He is observant, caring, and part of the group investigating Lucy's mysterious death.
Arthur	male	Lucy's fiancé who is deeply affected by her transformation into a vampire. He shows great courage when he performs the final act of destroying Lucy's undead form.
Van Helsing	*male	A Dutch professor and expert on supernatural matters who leads the investigation into Lucy's death. He is methodical, brave, and possesses deep religious faith.
Quincey Morris	male	A wealthy American who joins the group in their quest to destroy Lucy's vampire form. He is phlegmatic and brave, showing cool courage in the face of danger.
Lucy Westenra	*female	The deceased woman whose body is discovered empty in the tomb, later revealed to have been transformed into a vampire. She appears both beautiful and horrific during her vampiric form.
*Child	*male	A small child who is found near Lucy's tomb and later taken by the vampire. The child is described as being unharmed but moaning after being dropped.
