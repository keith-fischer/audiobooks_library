Dr. Seward	*male	A physician and member of the group investigating the vampire threat. He is practical, observant, and dedicated to helping others. He keeps a diary of the events.
Jonathan Harker	male	A young man who has recently married Mina. He is deeply devoted to his wife and suffers greatly from her affliction with the vampire. He is brave but overwhelmed by grief.
Mina Harker	female	Jonathan's wife, a kind and devoted woman who is the target of the vampire's attacks. She shows remarkable strength and compassion despite her suffering.
Van Helsing	*male	An elderly Dutch doctor who is the leader of the group investigating the vampire. He is wise, determined, and possesses extensive knowledge about supernatural matters.
Arthur Holmwood	*male	Lord Godalming, a wealthy young man who is deeply in love with Mina. He is brave and resourceful, taking charge of the group's actions during their hunt.
Quincey Morris	*male	A young American who is also deeply in love with Mina. He is adventurous, determined, and takes initiative during their quest to stop the vampire.
The Count	*male	The vampire antagonist, a centuries-old creature with supernatural powers. He is cunning, evil, and seeks to spread his influence through Mina.
