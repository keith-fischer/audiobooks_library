Dr. Seward	male	A physician and diary keeper who is the central figure in this chapter. He runs the asylum where Mr. Renfield is a patient, and serves as the leader of the group investigating Dracula. He is methodical, observant, and committed to his patients.
Mrs. Harker	female	The wife of Jonathan Harker, a key character who has been documenting events related to Dracula. She is intelligent, compassionate, and plays a crucial role in the investigation by organizing records of events.
Mr. Renfield	*male	A patient in Dr. Seward's asylum who is obsessed with consuming living creatures to prolong his life. He exhibits both madness and moments of apparent sanity, showing philosophical depth.
Lord Godalming	male	A wealthy British aristocrat who is involved in the investigation of Dracula. He is brave and committed to protecting Lucy, and later becomes part of the group hunting the vampire.
Mr. Morris	male	A friend of the group who is described as being from Texas. He is practical, direct in speech (laconic), and actively participates in the investigation of Dracula.
Professor Van Helsing	*male	A Dutch doctor and expert on supernatural phenomena who leads the investigation against Dracula. He is knowledgeable, wise, and determined to destroy the vampire.
Jonathan Harker	male	Mrs. Harker's husband who has been documenting events related to Dracula and is married to Mina. He is a key witness in the investigation.
