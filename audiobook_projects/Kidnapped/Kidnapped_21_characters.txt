Alan	male	A pock-marked, active man of about thirty-five, dressed in a feathered hat, French side-coat with silver buttons, red waistcoat and breeches of black shag. He is resourceful, clever with inventions like the fiery cross signal, and a skilled swordsman who teaches David. He is in hiding from red-coats with a 100 pound reward on his head, and is trying to get money from James for their escape to France.
David	male	A tall, strong lad of about eighteen wearing an old blue coat, Highland bonnet, homespun waistcoat and breeches. He is learning to use a sword from Alan but often gets the upper-hand in fishing. He has no beard and speaks like a Lowlander, making him easily recognizable by the authorities.
John Breck Maccoll	*male	A bouman (tenant farmer) of Appin who lives in Koalisnacoan. He is described as ragged, wild and bearded with smallpox scars, appearing dull and savage. He is a friend of Alan who helps him by delivering messages to James and bringing money, though he initially tries to cheat Alan out of his button.
*James	*male	A Stewart who is in hiding and has been arrested along with Alan. He is the one who needs to provide money for their escape, and has been imprisoned at Fort William under suspicion of complicity.
*Mrs. Stewart	*female	A woman who is related to Alan and James, likely a mother or aunt. She has sent money for their escape and is concerned about Alan's safety, warning that if he falls into enemy hands both he and James will be dead men.
*Duncan Stewart	*male	A man whose son Alan is, and who has a button that Alan uses in his signal. He is mentioned as having been killed or captured, and his son is now in hiding.
