Alan	male	A brave and fierce fighter who leads the defense of the round-house against attackers. He is described as invincible during battle, wielding a sword that runs blood to the hilt and showing great triumph after victory. He has a strong sense of honor, loves David like a brother, and is capable of both fierce combat and poetic expression.
*David	male	A young man who serves as Alan's companion and defender in the round-house. He is described as having a heavy heart, feeling fear during battle but ultimately firing pistols at attackers and contributing significantly to the defense. He is deeply affected by his actions in battle, experiencing nightmares and crying after victory.
*Mr. Shuan	male	The captain who leads the attackers against the round-house. He is killed by Alan in a sword fight, described as having blood pouring from his mouth and being dragged out of the house by enemies.
The captain	male	A naval officer who leads the attackers and is mentioned as being wounded by David's pistol shot. He shows a threatening demeanor when speaking to David and is described as having been killed by Alan.
*Mr. Riach	male	A seaman who, along with the captain, takes turns watching over the ship during the siege. He is mentioned as being involved in managing the crew after the battle.
