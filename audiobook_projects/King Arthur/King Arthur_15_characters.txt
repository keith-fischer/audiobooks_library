Sir Lancelot	male	The central knight of the Round Table, renowned for his prowess in battle and his affair with Queen Guinevere. He is portrayed as a noble but flawed hero who becomes embroiled in a war with King Arthur due to his relationship with the queen.
King Arthur	male	The rightful king of Britain, leader of the Round Table knights. He is torn between his love for Lancelot and his duty to uphold honor, ultimately leading to a devastating civil war.
Queen Guinevere	female	The queen of Britain and wife of King Arthur. She is the catalyst for much of the conflict, accused of dishonoring her husband and ultimately rescued by Lancelot.
Sir Gawain	male	King Arthur's nephew and a knight of the Round Table. He becomes Lancelot's sworn enemy after believing Lancelot killed his brothers Sir Gareth and Sir Gaheris in battle.
*Sir Agravaine	male	A knight of the Round Table and brother of Sir Gawain. He is killed by Lancelot during a confrontation in the queen's chamber.

*Sir Bors	male	A knight of the Round Table and Lancelot's close friend. He supports Lancelot in his battles against Arthur's forces.
