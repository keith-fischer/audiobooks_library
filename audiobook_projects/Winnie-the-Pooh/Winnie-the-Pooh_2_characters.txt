*Winnie-the-Pooh	male	A bear who is known by his friends as Pooh. He is proud, likes to hum, and enjoys honey and condensed milk. He gets stuck in Rabbit's hole while visiting.
Rabbit	male	A rabbit who lives in a hole in the forest and is visited by Pooh. He is cautious about visitors, likes to eat, and helps free Pooh from his predicament.
Christopher Robin	*male	A young boy who lives at the other end of the forest and helps Rabbit rescue Pooh. He is kind-hearted, loving towards Pooh, and makes jokes about the situation.
