Mina Murray	female	The narrator of the journal entries, a young woman staying in Whitby with Lucy Westenra. She is concerned about her husband Jonathan's absence and worries about Lucy's sleepwalking habits.
Lucy Westenra	female	Mina's friend, described as sweet and lovely with a beautiful colour since arriving in Whitby. She is engaged to be married to Arthur Holmwood and has a habit of sleepwalking.
Jonathan Harker	*male	Mina's husband who has not written to her for a month, causing her anxiety. He is mentioned as being in Castle Dracula and planning to return home.
Mr. Swales	*male	An elderly local man who is skeptical about legends and tombstones. He tells stories about Whitby's history, including the whale-fishing days.
Dr. Seward	*male	A physician who keeps a diary about his patient Renfield. He is studying the case of a zoöphagous (life-eating) maniac.
Renfield	*male	A patient in Dr. Seward's care who has an obsession with consuming living creatures to absorb their life force, starting with flies and progressing to spiders, birds, and eventually cats.
Mrs. Westenra	female	Lucy's mother, who is concerned about her daughter's sleepwalking and mentions that Lucy's father had the same habit.
Arthur Holmwood	*male	Lucy's fiancé, the Hon. Arthur Holmwood, only son of Lord Godalming. He is expected to arrive soon in Whitby.
Mr. Hawkins	*male	A kind man who sends Mina a letter from Jonathan, though the letter is brief and dated from Castle Dracula.
Lord Godalming	*male	Arthur Holmwood's father, who is not well and requires Arthur to visit him urgently.
Coastguard	*male	A local man who talks with Mina and notices a strange Russian ship approaching the harbor.
