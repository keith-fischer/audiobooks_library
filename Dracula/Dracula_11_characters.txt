Lucy Westenra	female	The main protagonist, a young woman who falls ill and is cared for by Dr. Van Helsing and Dr. Seward. She experiences sleeplessness, fear of sleep, and supernatural occurrences related to vampirism.
Dr. Van Helsing	male	A Dutch physician who is a specialist in the supernatural and becomes Lucy's primary caregiver. He is knowledgeable about vampires and takes charge of her treatment.
Dr. Seward	male	A physician and colleague of Van Helsing who keeps a diary documenting Lucy's illness. He is also involved in the investigation of supernatural events.
Mrs. Westenra	female	Lucy's mother, who is concerned about her daughter's health and inadvertently interferes with the treatment by removing flowers from Lucy's room.
Arthur Holmwood	male	Lucy's fiancé, mentioned as being kind and concerned for her wellbeing. He is a significant figure in Lucy's life.
Thomas Bilder	male	The keeper of the wolf section at the Zoölogical Gardens who tells his story about the escaped wolf Bersicker.
Mrs. Tom	*female	Thomas Bilder's wife, who is hospitable and supportive of her husband.
Bersicker	*male	A grey wolf at the Zoölogical Gardens who escapes and later returns, becoming central to the supernatural events.
The Interviewer	*male	A reporter from the Pall Mall Gazette who interviews Thomas Bilder about the escaped wolf.
