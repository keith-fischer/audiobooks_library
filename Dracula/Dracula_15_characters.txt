Dr. Seward	*male	A physician and diary keeper who is initially skeptical of Van Helsing's theories about vampirism. He is loyal to his friend Arthur and becomes convinced of the supernatural events after witnessing Lucy's coffin being empty. He is torn between his scientific mind and growing belief in the supernatural.
Van Helsing	*male	A Dutch physician and expert on monsters who is determined to prove Lucy is a vampire. He is methodical, knowledgeable about the supernatural, and shows great dedication to his cause. He has a strong sense of duty and is willing to make personal sacrifices for what he believes is right.
Lucy Westenra	female	A young woman who has died and become a vampire (Un-Dead). She was bitten by a vampire while sleepwalking, which caused her death and transformation. Her body appears beautiful but is actually undead.
Arthur Holmwood	*male	Lord Godalming, Lucy's fiancé who is devastated by her death. He initially refuses to believe Van Helsing's claims about Lucy being a vampire and is deeply opposed to any desecration of her grave. He becomes convinced after witnessing the events.
Quincey Morris	*male	An American friend of Arthur who is willing to help in the fight against vampirism. He shows trust in Van Helsing and supports his actions, even when they seem extreme.
Dr. Vincent	*male	A physician at the North Hospital who treats a child with bite wounds similar to Lucy's. He attributes the bites to animals like bats or rats, showing a more scientific approach to the mysterious occurrences.
The Child	*male	A young child who was bitten by a vampire, showing wounds similar to Lucy's. The child is found in the hospital and later discovered to be unharmed, suggesting he was not killed by the vampire.
