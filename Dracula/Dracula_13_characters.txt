Dr. Seward	male	A physician and diary keeper who is deeply involved in the supernatural events surrounding Lucy Westenra's death. He is a close friend of Van Helsing and Arthur Holmwood, and serves as a witness to the strange occurrences. He is portrayed as compassionate yet pragmatic.
Van Helsing	male	A foreign doctor and lawyer who is a key figure in investigating the supernatural events. He is knowledgeable, mysterious, and deeply committed to protecting Lucy and her family. He shows great emotional investment in the case.
Lucy Westenra	female	A young woman who dies from a supernatural cause, becoming a vampire. She is described as extremely beautiful and beloved by her family and friends.
Arthur Holmwood	male	Lucy's fiancé who is deeply affected by her death. He is portrayed as devoted, noble, and emotionally vulnerable during this tragic time.
Mrs. Westenra	female	Lucy's mother who dies before Lucy, leaving her daughter as the sole heir to her estate.
Mina Harker	female	A woman who becomes Mrs. Jonathan Harker after marriage. She is a journal keeper and plays an important role in uncovering the truth about Lucy's death.
Jonathan Harker	male	Mina's husband, a solicitor who has recently returned from abroad. He suffers from nervous problems and is deeply affected by Lucy's death.
Quincey Morris	male	A friend of Arthur Holmwood who accompanies him back to Ring. He is described as a fine fellow and moral Viking, showing deep concern for Lucy's death.
Mr. Marquand	*male	A solicitor who handles the legal affairs of Mrs. Westenra's estate and provides information about Lucy's will.
The Undertaker	*male	A professional who prepares bodies for burial and creates the funeral arrangements for Lucy and her mother.
The Woman	*female	A mysterious figure who steals the golden crucifix from Lucy's body during the night, showing devotion to her deceased mistress.
The Dark Man	*male	A mysterious stranger observed by Jonathan Harker, described as having a cruel and sensual face with pointed teeth.
The Children	*male	Young children who are reported missing and injured in Hampstead, allegedly lured away by a 'bloofer lady'.
