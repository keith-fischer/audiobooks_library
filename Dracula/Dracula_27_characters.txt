Mina Harker	female	The protagonist and narrator of the story, a woman who is married to Jonathan Harker. She is described as beautiful and strong-willed, enduring great suffering and fear while trying to save her husband from the vampire Count Dracula. She is deeply religious, devoted to her husband and family.
Jonathan Harker	male	Mina's husband, a young English solicitor who travels to Transylvania to assist Count Dracula with property transactions. He becomes a prisoner of the vampire and later fights against him to save Mina.
Abraham Van Helsing	male	A Dutch physician and expert on supernatural phenomena who leads the fight against Count Dracula. He is intelligent, determined, and deeply committed to protecting others from the vampire threat.
Dr. John Seward	male	A physician and friend of Jonathan Harker who becomes involved in the fight against Dracula. He is a close companion to Van Helsing and helps coordinate efforts to stop the vampire.
Quincey Morris	male	A wealthy American friend of Jonathan Harker who joins the quest to destroy Dracula. He is brave, determined, and ultimately sacrifices his life in the final confrontation with the vampire.
Lord Godalming	male	A British aristocrat and friend of Jonathan Harker who joins the fight against Dracula. He is courageous and leads efforts to track down and destroy the vampire.
Count Dracula	*male	The main antagonist, a vampire who terrorizes Mina and Jonathan. He is described as pale with red eyes, possessing supernatural powers and a vindictive nature.
The Three Vampire Women	*female	Three female vampires who appear in the castle and attempt to seduce Mina. They are described as beautiful but dangerous, with red eyes, white teeth, and voluptuous features.
