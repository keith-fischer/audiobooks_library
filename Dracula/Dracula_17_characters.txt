Dr. Seward	male	A physician who keeps a phonograph diary and works with Van Helsing to investigate supernatural events. He is compassionate, thoughtful, and deeply involved in the case against Count Dracula.
Mina Harker	female	Jonathan Harker's wife, a strong and determined woman who keeps her own diary and works alongside Dr. Seward to uncover the truth about Count Dracula. She is brave, intelligent, and deeply devoted to her husband.
Jonathan Harker	male	Mina's husband, a young man who travels to Transylvania and later Whitby to investigate Count Dracula. He is clever, energetic, and shows remarkable courage in facing supernatural threats.
Lucy Westenra	*female	A young woman who becomes a victim of Count Dracula and whose death sets the events in motion. She is described as dear, sweet, and beloved by those around her.
Lord Godalming	male	A friend of Lucy's who becomes involved in the fight against Dracula. He is emotional and deeply affected by Lucy's death, showing great loyalty to her memory.
Mr. Morris	male	Another friend of Lucy's who joins the fight against Dracula. He is brave, selfless, and shows deep emotional strength in his grief over Lucy's death.
Professor Van Helsing	male	An expert on supernatural matters who leads the investigation against Count Dracula. He is wise, knowledgeable, and instrumental in understanding the vampire threat.
Renfield	*male	A patient at Dr. Seward's asylum who exhibits strange behavior related to the vampire threat and serves as an indicator of Count Dracula's presence.
Billington	male	A local official in Whitby who provides information to Jonathan Harker about the arrival of boxes from Varna.
Billington junior	male	The son of Billington, who meets Jonathan Harker at the train station in Whitby and provides hospitality.
