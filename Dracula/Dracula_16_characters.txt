Dr. Seward	male	A physician and one of the main protagonists who keeps a diary. He is observant, caring, and serves as a key member of the group investigating supernatural events.
Van Helsing	male	A Dutch doctor and expert on the supernatural who leads the investigation. He is methodical, knowledgeable about ancient lore, and displays great determination in fighting evil.
Arthur	male	Lucy Westenra's fiancé who is deeply affected by her transformation into a vampire. He shows great courage in the final confrontation and demonstrates his love for Lucy.
Quincey Morris	male	A wealthy American who joins the group in their quest. He is brave, practical, and shows a calm demeanor even under extreme circumstances.
Lucy Westenra	female	The main victim who becomes a vampire. She is described as beautiful and pure in life, but transformed into a creature of evil and temptation.
The Child	*male	A young child who was taken by Lucy's vampire form. The child is described as being found unharmed and later returned to safety.
