Dr. Seward	*male	A physician and member of the group investigating Count Dracula. He is practical, observant, and dedicated to stopping the vampire. He accompanies Mina on her journey to Dracula's castle.
Mrs. Harker	female	Mina Harker, Jonathan's wife. She is intelligent and determined, serving as a key figure in the investigation through her hypnotic abilities and later joining Van Helsing on their journey to Dracula's castle.
Jonathan Harker	male	Mina's husband, a solicitor who was imprisoned by Count Dracula in his castle. He is brave and devoted to his wife, participating actively in the hunt for Dracula.
Lord Godalming	male	A wealthy nobleman who leads the expedition to pursue Dracula. He is resourceful, determined, and provides financial support for their mission.
Quincey Morris	male	A wealthy American who joins the hunt for Dracula. He is adventurous, brave, and skilled with firearms.
Dr. Van Helsing	*male	A Dutch physician and expert on supernatural matters who leads the investigation against Count Dracula. He is wise, experienced, and determined to destroy the vampire.
Captain Donelson	*male	The captain of the ship _Czarina Catherine_ who transported Count Dracula from London to Galatz. He is superstitious but practical, and was initially suspicious of the box.
Immanuel Hildesheim	*male	A Hebrew businessman in Galatz who received the box from London for Count Dracula. He acted as an intermediary in the vampire's transport.
Petrof Skinsky	*male	A Slovak trader who was supposed to receive the box from Hildesheim and arrange transportation. He was murdered, possibly by Dracula.
Mina Harker	female	Jonathan's wife, who becomes a key figure in the investigation through her hypnotic abilities and later joins Van Helsing on their journey to Dracula's castle.
